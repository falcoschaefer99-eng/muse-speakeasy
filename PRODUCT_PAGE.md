## Title

MUSE SpeakEasy - Push-to-Talk Voice Input That Runs on Your Machine

## Short Description

Press one key. Speak. Your words appear wherever you type. 100% local, 99 languages, Mac + Windows.

## Full Description

**Stop Typing. Start Talking. Your Voice, Your Machine, Your Privacy.**

SpeakEasy turns any text field on your computer into a microphone. Press one key, speak naturally, and your words appear — terminal, editor, browser, chat, anywhere. Everything runs locally. Nothing ever leaves your machine.

---

**Core Features**

- **One-key voice input** — press to start, press again to stop. Escape to cancel
- **Ramble-friendly** — talk for up to 5 minutes. Smart silence detection knows when you're thinking vs. when you're done
- **99 languages** — English, German, Spanish, Japanese, and 95+ more
- **Mac + Windows** — full support for both platforms, one-click installers included
- **Zero background footprint** — does nothing until you press the key
- **Configurable** — adjust sensitivity, recording duration, transcription quality, and language to match your setup

---

**Your Data, Your Control**

100% local and private. All transcription happens on YOUR machine. Nothing uploaded. Nothing tracked. No cloud. No subscription. No account required.

---

**Perfect For**

- **Developers** who think faster than they type — dictate ideas, comments, and messages without breaking flow
- **Writers** who want to capture thoughts instantly — ramble now, edit later
- **Accessibility** — reduce keyboard strain by speaking instead of typing
- **Terminal power users** who want voice input without leaving the command line

---

**What's Included**

- Complete cross-platform voice engine
- Mac and Windows hotkey configurations
- One-click installers for both platforms
- Full setup guide with troubleshooting

---

**Requirements**

- Python 3.10+
- A microphone
